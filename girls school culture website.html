Positive Girls School Culture

  • Peer support

    • At all occasions, they cheer for one another by shouting out loud.

  • Feminism

    • They are very vocal about female rights



Weird Girls School Cultures

  • Gossiping
  • Fangirling over seniors
  • A lot of girls drama

Benefits of Girls School

  • Academic success

    • Girls school are more strict academic-wise, hence students have to be disciplined to achieve good academic results. In addition, students in girls schools are also competitive, which encourage them to work hard to achieve better academic results.

  • Increased career aspirations

    • Some girls school have programmes to increase students' awareness on various occupations. For example, an alumnae camp where some alumni came back to share about their respective careers and the subjects required. It benefitted students greatly.

  • Identity awareness and confidence

    • In an all-girls environment, students feel more open to share their ideas and thoughts. This makes them more confident to talk in public in the future.
